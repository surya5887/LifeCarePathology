Our Story

Life Care Pathology Lab was established with a vision to provide high-quality diagnostic services to the people of Asara and surrounding areas. We believe that accurate diagnostics are the foundation of good healthcare.

Located at Rikshaw Stand, Asara, Uttar Pradesh, our lab is equipped with modern instruments and staffed by experienced pathologists and technicians who are committed to delivering precise results.

Over the years, we have served thousands of patients with a wide range of pathology tests including blood tests, urine analysis, thyroid profiles, diabetes screening, and much more.
